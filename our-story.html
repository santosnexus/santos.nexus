Our Journey

Seventeen years. One family.

Selected milestones from the Santos King story — the moments that defined who we are today.

2009
Santos King is founded

A government-recognized tour company opens in Kochi, Kerala. The first Santos Travel office in Aluva begins serving travellers across India.

2014
Tour de Kerala

Our first major sports event — a 90-km cycle marathon along the Sabarimala road. The beginning of TDK Sports.

2016
Kochi Swimathon is born

The first Federal Bank Kochi Swimathon Ultra+ — now India's largest open-water swimathon, attracting thousands of swimmers from across India and the GCC.

2017
Duskathon launches

The first Kochi Duskathon — a barefoot beach run timed to the sunset. TDK Sports becomes a recognisable Kerala brand.

2018
Santos Care begins

Heal India Medi Tourism (later Santos Care) is established to serve international patients seeking quality, affordable healthcare in India.

2021
10,000+ patients served

Santos Care crosses a major milestone — over 10,000 international patients facilitated through 150+ JCI and NABH accredited hospitals.

2022
Paddlers Meetup

A new TDK Sports event — bringing together paddlers and adventure water sports enthusiasts in Kerala.

2025
7,000+ swimmers at Swimathon 2025

TDK Sports continues to grow the Swimathon into a national event, with participation from swimmers across India, the GCC, and beyond.

2026
The Santos.Nexus era begins

The family comes together under one digital roof. Santos.Nexus — the umbrella site and the technology brand — launches.
