Our Events

Events that move people.

From open-water swims to barefoot beach runs, TDK Sports events are designed to challenge, connect, and celebrate.

Annual · Since 2016

Federal Bank Kochi Swimathon Ultra+

India's largest open-water swimathon. Thousands of swimmers across age groups, all swimming the Periyar river in Aluva, Kochi.

2018–2019

Kochi Duskathon

A barefoot beach run timed to the Kerala sunset. Kuzhuppilly to Cherai — one of the longest and most picturesque beaches in Ernakulam.

2021

Paddlers Meetup

Bringing together paddlers and adventure water-sports enthusiasts in Kerala. A community-first event for the active traveller.

2014

Tour de Kerala

A 90-km cycle marathon along the Sabarimala road. The event that started TDK Sports — proof that Kerala was ready for endurance sports.
